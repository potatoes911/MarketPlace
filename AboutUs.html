One man's trash is another man's treasure.

An interactive marketplace designed for sharing, renting, and finding a new home for items.

🗑️

The Problem We Face

Every day, items that are no longer valued are either binned or sent straight to landfill. Many people have appliances, furniture, and other goods that they no longer use or only required for a one-time occasion. Because of this, perfectly good items gather dust or go to waste unnecessarily.

♻️

Our Solution

We created a central location for individuals to list their items to be purchased cheaply, given away for free, or rented. By improving the volume, velocity, and variety of options available to consumers, this marketplace reduces the amount of junk ending up in landfills.

🏠

Helping New Homeowners

Setting up a new home can be incredibly expensive. This platform serves as an item-sharing and sales hub where new homeowners can find affordable, gently-used items that are still in fantastic condition, helping them save money while living sustainably.

🛠️

Leasing and One-Time Use

Sometimes you only need a specific tool once, like an expensive hand drill for a quick home repair. Purchasing it brand new wouldn't be financially viable. Secondhand allows individuals to lease equipment, making high-quality tools accessible without the steep price tag.

💻

An Enterprise Computing Project

This website was developed as an Enterprise Computing assessment task aimed at identifying a real-world problem and addressing it through an enterprise project. Secondhand supports the idea of selling cheap "junk" so others can use it, proving that with the right platform, sharing resources benefits both the community and the environment.
