How It Works

A quick guide to navigating, trading, and engaging with the Secondhand community.

🔍

Browsing the Marketplace

Navigate to the Market page to view all available listings. Use the search bar at the top to quickly find specific items by title, category, or description.

Keep an eye out for colored tags on the listings: Sale indicates items to purchase, Free means the item is completely free to take, and Borrow means the item is available for temporary loan (great for expensive, one-time use tools).

📸

Listing an Item

Have an item gathering dust? Click the green "＋ List an Item" button on the Market page. Fill out the details such as Title, Category, and Condition.

You can upload up to 5 photos to showcase your item. Don't forget to set your listing type (Sale, Free, or Borrow) and include clear contact instructions and pickup locations in your description.

✉️

Contacting and Commenting

Click on any item card to open its detailed view. If you are interested, use the "Contact Seller" button to immediately initiate an email or phone call with the owner based on their provided contact info.

Have a general question about dimensions or condition? Scroll to the bottom of the item details and leave a public Comment for the seller to answer.

📊

Community Polls

We want the marketplace to work for you. Navigate to the Polls tab to see what items or features the community is looking for.

You can vote on active polls, comment your thoughts, or click "＋ Create Poll" to ask the community a question of your own (e.g., "Would anyone be interested in a community lawnmower pool?"). You can provide up to 6 custom options for people to vote on.
